My Favorite Constellations

Here is a list and discription of all of my favorite constellations

Orion

  • The constellation is named after Orion, a legendary hunter in Greek Mythology.
  • There are many greek myths about how Orion came to be in the sky.
    1. In one myth,it is said that Artemis fell in love with Orion and was tricked by her brother Apollo into killing Orion. Arrtemis begs Ascelpius to heal Orion but Zeus kills Ascelpius before he can heal Orion which leads Artemis to set Orion's image in the stars.
    2. In another, Orion is banished to the sky for boasting about how many animals he would kill, his overconfidence leading to his demise.
    3. In a third myth, Orion is too confident in his hunting abilities and declares that he would kill every animal in the world. This angers Gaea, the goddess of the earth, and she sends a scorpion to kill him. When Orion dies, Zeus turns him into a constellation, along with the scorpion that killed him creating both the Orion and Scorpius constellation.
  • Orion is easily identifiable by "Orions Belt" which is a cluster of three stars that form Orion's Belt in the constellation.
  • Orion is the first constellation that I discovered in the night sky, it is the reason I delved into a deep fascination with stars and constellations.
